Project Peakbagger

What is Peakbagging? Peakbagging is the pursuit of summiting multiple mountains from a specific list, Whether they're the tallest in a region, the most prominent, or simply iconic. It's a blend of adventure, discipline, and obsession: a minimalist, goal-driven form of mountaineering where every summit is a step toward completion.
